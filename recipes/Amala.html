Amala

A beautiful plate of amala and ewedu

Amala is a food native to the yoruba tribe of nigeria made from grounding dried yam peels it is originally a powder put into a boiling pot of steaming hot water in which it then solidifies and is eaten as a staple.

Ingredients

  • Yam peel flour
  • Hot water
  • jews mallow
  • pepper
  • assorted beef

Steps

  1. Boil the water
  2. put in the yam flour stir till smooth and put aside
  3. Cook the jews mallow and shred it up, set aside
  4. cook a stew with the assorted meat and peppers
  5. Voila! dish your food
