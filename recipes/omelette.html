A beautiful plate of omelette

Omelette is a sweet tasty egg dish easily made in a short while common as a breakfast in europe

Ingredients

  • egg
  • sauce
  • spices
  • peppers

Steps

  1. Heat oil
  2. break the egg
  3. Dice the peppers till fine
  4. mix the eggs and the peppers with the spices
  5. Put all in the oil fry till golden brown.
  6. Voila! dish your food
