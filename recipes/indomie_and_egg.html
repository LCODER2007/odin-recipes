Indomie and egg

A beautiful plate of Indomie and eggs

Indomie is a delicious staple in nigeria easy and fast to cook while being so taty are its main marketing points

Ingredients

  • Indomie
  • Hot water
  • eggs
  • pepper
  • assorted beef

Steps

  1. Boil the water
  2. put in the indomie and the egg still in shell
  3. Cook the peppers and shred it up, set aside
  4. put all together cook until dry, remove egg shell
  5. Voila! dish your food
